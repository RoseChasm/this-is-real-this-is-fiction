From George Eliot to Rabindranath Tagore, the writers I love were always more than writers. They were politicians and painters, doctors and philosophers, travelers and Hollywood sellouts. As a writer I've always tried to be many things so I can write about all of them. I'm not hauled up in some MFA program--I'm out in LA trying to make money writing about real things. Also known as being a journalist.

So I've worked for political campaigns, game design studios, NPR and travel blogs. I've built websites (like this one) written plays, designed graphic novel websites and book covers. I've gotten struck by lightning and stuck in a psych ward, and got internationally famous on CNN. I write made-up stories and true ones too, and right now I'm writing a novel that's a mixture of both. I sometimes go by the name RoseChasm (short for my anagram-name Rosalie C. Chasm) on the internet.

That's why I've called my site this is real this is fiction; real stories feed my fiction, and so I don't try to sepearate what I do from what I make. I'm trying to create one solid, tangled messy life out of these many different roles, and so far it's been fun. So check out my authors, my stories, and my works in progress.
